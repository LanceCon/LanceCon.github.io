Event Force

Event Force is the brainchild of Lance Connolly and Sean Binder, formulated in early 2013, the company continues to adapt to the much evolving event industry of Cape Town. We saw a gap in the market and decided to tap into it with full force. With over 20 years experience in the industry, we strive on providing service that is unknown in Cape Town, service that will keep our clients happy and in turn build a strong foundation in keeping those clients coming back to us time and time again. Event Force core business is supplying professional event assistants and staff to event companies in Cape Town. We supply experienced production managers who are safety-conscious, efficient and hardworking..
